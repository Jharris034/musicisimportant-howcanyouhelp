How You Can Make A Differencee

Talk To School Administration

You SHould first take your Ideas to your child's School Administration, doing this will show the school staff that you care about the Music Deparment, particurally in your child's School.

,

Talk TO School District Board

Next Talk to your School District Board Members, share your concern with them, remind them how important Music is for our Children. This will have the same effect, it will show them that Parents care for Music in Schools.

Show You Care

Another Way you can help is by simply donating to your Schools Music Department. This will show your Music Teacher that you care about what they are doing, that you understand what music is doing for our children, and that you understand, Yes, Music is Important!
